About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Computational Biophysics at Purdue University, working with Prof. Carol Beth Post. My research integrates molecular simulations and data-driven modeling to study conformational transitions in proteins, kinase regulation, and the impact of post-translational modifications on structural dynamics.
